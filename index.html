Welcome to Robobattle Royale
You are about to battle robots to the robo-death.
Instructions
  • Choose one of the four robots as your fighter.
  • Battle the remaining three robots by clicking the Robo-Attack button.
  • Your Attack Power (AP) will increase with every attack.
  • After you attack, the enemy will damage you by their Counter-Attack Power (CAP).
  • Whichever robot runs out of Health Points (HP) first loses.
  • Defeat all three enemy robots in a single life to win the game.
